# 📊 Retail Revenue Loss Analysis

## 🔍 Overview

Businesses often focus on increasing sales, but high revenue does not always guarantee profitability.
This project analyzes retail data to identify **why losses occur despite strong sales performance** and provides actionable insights to improve profitability.

---

## 🎯 Objective

* Analyze sales, profit, and loss trends
* Identify key drivers of revenue loss
* Evaluate the impact of discounts on profitability
* Provide data-driven business recommendations

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies

* **Excel** – Data cleaning and exploratory analysis
* **SQL** – Data querying and aggregation
* **Power BI** – Interactive dashboard and visualization

---

## 📂 Dataset

* **Name:** SuperStore Orders Dataset
* **File:** `data/SuperStoreOrders.csv`
* Contains order-level data including:

  * Sales
  * Profit
  * Discount
  * Category
  * Region

---

## 📊 Dashboard Highlights

* KPI Cards: Total Sales, Total Profit, Total Loss
* Profit analysis by Category
* Discount impact on Profitability
* Category-wise performance comparison

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

* High discount orders are the primary driver of revenue loss
* Technology category generates the highest profit
* Increased discount levels lead to negative profit margins
* Some categories generate high sales but low profitability

---

## 🧮 SQL Analysis

* Aggregated total sales, profit, and loss using `SUM()`
* Performed category-level analysis using `GROUP BY`
* Identified loss-making segments
* Analyzed the relationship between discount and profit

---

## 📈 Excel Analysis

* Used pivot tables for summarization
* Performed category-wise and discount analysis
* Built initial dashboard for validation

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Optimize discount strategy to prevent losses
* Focus on high-margin product categories
* Monitor discount vs profit relationship regularly
* Implement data-driven pricing strategies

---

## 📌 Conclusion

The analysis reveals that excessive discounting is a major cause of revenue loss.
By controlling discount levels and focusing on profitability, businesses can significantly improve their financial performance.
