# Retail Revenue Loss Analysis

## Problem Statement

The retail business generates strong sales but faces inconsistent profitability.
This project analyzes where revenue loss is occurring and identifies the root causes.

---

## Objective

* Analyze sales, profit, and loss trends
* Identify drivers of revenue loss
* Provide actionable insights

---

## Tools Used

* Excel (Data Cleaning & Analysis)
* SQL (Data Querying)
* Power BI (Dashboard & Visualization)

---

## Dataset

* Dataset Name: SuperStore Orders Dataset
* File: data/SuperStoreOrders.csv

---

## Key Insights

* High discount orders are the main cause of losses
* Technology category generates highest profit
* High discounts lead to negative profit
* Some categories have high sales but low profit

---

## SQL Analysis

* Used SUM() for total sales and profit
* Used GROUP BY for category analysis
* Identified loss-making segments
* Analyzed discount impact

---

## Excel Analysis

* Used pivot tables for category-wise analysis
* Performed initial data validation
* Created summary dashboard

---

## Power BI Dashboard

* Created KPI cards for Sales, Profit, Loss
* Built visuals for category-wise performance
* Analyzed discount impact on profit
* Designed interactive dashboard

---

## Business Recommendations

* Reduce high discount levels
* Improve pricing strategy
* Focus on profitable categories
* Monitor discount impact regularly

---

## Conclusion

Excessive discounting is the major reason for revenue loss.
Optimizing discount strategies can improve overall profitability.
